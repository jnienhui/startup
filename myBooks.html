Red Rising

by Pierce Brown

Red Rising is the first book in the Red Rising Trilogy. This novel is of the dystopian science-fiction genre and takes place in the future on the planet of Mars. On the planet of Mars and this current time-period there exists a caste system with the Golds at the top, the greys as security guards, the reds at the very bottom and others. The novel follows the struggles of Darrow, a Red, and the protagonist of this story as he fights against the caste system. The first novel follows his story as he infiltrates the Gold society with the help of the resistence against this system.
